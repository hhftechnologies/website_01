Jan Diehm

Jan Diehm is a Sr. Journalist-Engineer at The Pudding. She appreciates the finer things in life: LEGO sets, southern delicacies like pimento cheese and fried green tomatoes, and vintage Britney Spears. You can follow them on Twitter or check out their website. They have worked on 21 stories to date. Explore them all below.
